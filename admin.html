Arrival Notifications
Control which emails are sent automatically when a visitor checks in. SMTP must be configured on the server for emails to send.

Send visitor check-in confirmation
Sends a branded confirmation email to the visitor on check-in. Requires the visitor to provide their email address.

Sends a summary email at a set time listing all visitors who have not checked out. Only sends if there are unresolved check-ins.
